SharePoint keeps track of your file changes for you. If you ever make a mistake, delete something by accident, or need an older version, SharePoint has your back.

  • Version history lets you open, compare, or restore older versions of any file.
  • You can review who changed what and when ‚Äî useful for collaboration or auditing.
  • Restoring a version doesn‚Äôt delete history; it simply becomes the newest version.
  • Check-out is optional and used when you want full control while making changes.

Lists ‚Äì simple structured data

Lists help teams track information in a clean, organised way. Think of them as smart online tables where everyone can update information without needing Excel files floating around.

  • Lists are great for things like tasks, issues, contacts, equipment tracking, requests, or sign-ups.

When to use a list

Lists shine when your team needs to track information that changes often. Instead of passing around spreadsheets, SharePoint lists keep everything in one shared place where everyone sees the latest version.
